Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 27, 2025

At MoodTask, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Mood entries, notes, and music habits stored in your browser’s local storage.
  • Usage Data: Anonymized data like IP address and pages visited.
  • Third-Party Analytics: We use Google Analytics for anonymized usage data.
  • Third-Party Embeds: YouTube embeds on the Mood Music page may collect data per their privacy policy.
  • Cookies: Used to remember preferences; you can opt-out via browser settings.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide personalized recommendations.
  • Track mood and activity history locally.
  • Improve our services via anonymized analytics.

Data Security

We use reasonable measures to protect your data, but no system is completely secure. Most data is stored locally in your browser.

Your Rights

  • Clear your data by clearing your browser cache.
  • Opt-out of cookies via browser settings.
  • Contact us at privacy@moodtask.org.

Children’s Privacy

MoodTask is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect their data.

Third-Party Links

We link to third-party sites (e.g., YouTube, Online Therapy). Review their privacy policies before use.

Limitation of Liability

MoodTask is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Use at your own risk.

Contact Us

For questions, email privacy@moodtask.org.
